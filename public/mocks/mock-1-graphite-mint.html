Account safety

Your LinkedIn account is the asset.
We engineered around it.

Most scrapers get accounts restricted within weeks. Coldcast never touches your password and never leaves your machine — which is why our ban count is still zero.

Other scrapers

Cloud bots on borrowed sessions

The typical tool logs into your account from a datacenter and hammers the API until LinkedIn notices. It always notices.

  • Stored passwords — your credentials sit on someone else's server.
  • Datacenter IPs — a login from a cloud region you've never visited.
  • Robotic request pace — thousands of calls a minute, trivially fingerprinted.
Result: restricted and banned accounts.
The Coldcast way

Your browser. Your session. Human pace.

Coldcast drives the browser you're already logged into, from your own machine — indistinguishable from you doing the work by hand, just 100x faster to a CSV.

  • No password ever shared — we ride your existing session, nothing stored.
  • Your own IP and fingerprint — same device LinkedIn already trusts.
  • Human-pace pagination — throttled to look exactly like a person browsing.
Result: 0 bans across 10,000+ users.
