What I'd watch for

A and B both put two greens inside one row — an icon 11px from its label, in different accents. That's the pattern we've spent all day removing everywhere else (the mint tile beside the lime badge, the mint chart beside the lime button). Judge them on that: does the row read as a decision, or as two colours that couldn't agree?
The other thing to check is the top-right. In C, Free trial is the only loud thing on screen. In A and B, six lime glyphs (or six lime labels) are competing with it from the panel directly below. A menu is chrome — you read it and move on. The button is the point.
